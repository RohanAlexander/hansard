Arnold Wienholt (1877-1940): Elected 1919
Born: 25 November 1877, Goomburra, Queensland.
Career: Educated Eton College, England. Grazier, Darling Downs. 
Military Service 1899-1902, 1914-16. Author.
State politics: Qld MLA for Fassifern 1909-13.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Weinholt was endorsed both by the PPU and the Nationalist Party. He 
did not join the Country Party in 1920 but often voted with them. He 
retired in 1922.
Later career: Qld MLA Fassifern for 1930-35. Farmer and author. 
Military Service 1939-40.
Died 10 September 1940 (Killed in acton, Abyssinia.)
