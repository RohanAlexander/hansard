INDI, Vic                                            40,336 enrolled
====================================================================
North-east Victoria: Benalla, Croydon, Wangaratta, Warburton
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1925 two-party majority: Country Party over ALP 06.7
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Paul JONES                      ALP      Unopposed 
--------------------------------------------------------------------
The sitting member since 1919, Robert Cook (Country Party), failed 
to lodge his nomination papers. This is the only occasion in federal 
parliamentary history that a member has lost his seat in this 
manner. Cook's lapse was particularly momentous, since the Bruce 
Government lost office in 1929 after being defeated in committee by 
one vote. 
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Paul Jones (1878-1972): Elected 1928
Born: 1878, Gaffney's Creek, Victoria.
Career: Educated South Melbourne College. Goldminer, teacher. 
Studied University of Melbourne, MA.
