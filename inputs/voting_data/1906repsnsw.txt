PARRAMATTA, NSW                                      29,549 enrolled
====================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------
1903 majority: Free Trade over Independent 30.1
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Hon Joseph COOK *               AS     Unopposed
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Joseph Cook (1860-1947): Elected 1901, 1903, 1906
Minister for Defence from 2 June 1909
Leader of the Opposition 17 November 1908 to 26 May 1909
Leader of the Anti-Socialists 17 November 1908 to 26 May 1909
Deputy Leader of the Liberal Party from 26 May 1909
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Cook was elected Leader of the Anti-Socialists following the 
resignation of Reid, principally because he was a negotiating 
partner acceptable to the Protectionists. When the parties fused he 
conceded the leadership of the new party to Deakin, even though he 
had many more followers.

John Christian Watson (1867-1941): Elected 1901, 1903, 1906
Leader of the Labour Party to 23 October 1907
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Watson resigned as Leader of the Labour Party partly due to ill-
health and partly due to party opposition to his close collaboration 
with Deakin. He retired in 1910.
Later career: Left ALP 1916 over conscription. Company director. One 
of the founders of Ampol and the NRMA.
Died 18 November 1941
