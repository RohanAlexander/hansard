Paul Meernaa Caedwalla Hasluck (1905-93): Elected 1949
Born: 1 April 1905, Perth.
Career: Educated Perth Modern School, University of Western 
Australia. Journalist. Lecturer and Reader in History University of 
Western Australia 1939-49. Seconded to Department of External 
Affairs 1941-47. Attended San Francisco UN Conference 1945. Author 
two volumes of official history, Australia in the War of 1939-45.

Hugh Alan Leslie (1900-74): Elected 1949
Born: 17 April 1900, Durban, South Africa.
Career: Educated private schools, South Africa. Journalist. Military
