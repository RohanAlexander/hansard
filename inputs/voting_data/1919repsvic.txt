> David Charles McGrath (1873-1934): Elected 1913, 1914, 1917. 
Defeated 1919
Later career: See Ballaarat by-election 1920. 
> Edwin Thomas Kerby (1885-1971): Elected 1919
Born: 12 April 1885, Ballaarat, Victoria.
Career: Educated Grenville College, Ballaarat. Mining engineer and 
electrical contractor. Military Service 1914-19.
Election declared void 2 June 1920 (see By-elections)
Later career: Businessman, mainly in aviation. Leader of RSL and 
other ex-service causes.
Died 5 July 1971
