Harold George Nelson (1881-1947): Elected 1922
Born: 1881, NSW
Career: Primary education. Laborer, miner, enginedriver, Queensland. 
To NT 1913. Union organiser. Secretary, Australian Workers Union, NT 
Workers Union. Father of John Nelson MP.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
The Northern Territory was part of South Australia 1901-03 and part 
of Grey, South Australia, 1903-11, but did not elect a member 
between the time of its transfer to the Commonwealth in 1911 and 
1922. The member could vote only on matters affecting the Territory.
