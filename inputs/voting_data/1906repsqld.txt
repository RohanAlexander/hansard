Andrew Fisher (1862-1928): Elected 1901, 1903, 1906
Prime Minister 13 November 1908 to 2 June 1909
Treasurer 13 November 1908 to 2 June 1909
Leader of the Opposition from 2 June 1909
Assistant Leader of the Labour Party to 30 October 1907
Leader of the Labour Party from 30 October 1907 (Australian Labour 
Party from 1908)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Fisher was elected leader of the Labour Party following the 
resignation of Watson. Following the breakdown of the informal 
coalition between the Protectionists and the ALP, resulting in the 
House carrying a vote of no-confidence in Deakin's government on 10
November 1908, he formed a minority government. He resigned office 
following the formation of the Liberal Party and the passage of a 
no-confidence motion in his government on 27 May 1909.
