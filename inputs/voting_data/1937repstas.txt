Joseph Aloysius Lyons (1867-1939): Elected 1929, 1931, 1934, 1937
Prime Minister to 7 April 1939
Treasurer to 7 April 1939
Vice-President of the Executive Council to 29 November 1937
Minister for Defence 20 November 1937 to 29 November 1937
Leader of the United Australia Party to 7 April 1939
Died 7 April 1939 (see By-elections)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Lyons suffered a heart attack while driving from Canberra to Sydney 
and died in hospital shortly after. He was the first Prime Minister 
to die in office.
