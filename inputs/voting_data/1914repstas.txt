TASMANIA
====================================================================

BASS, Tas                      22,318 enrolled, 17,639 (79.0%) voted
====================================================================
Northern Tasmania: Fingal, Launceston, Ringarooma, Scottsdale
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1913 two-party majority: ALP over Liberal 02.4
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Jens JENSEN *                   ALP     9,731   56.0  (+03.6)
Alexander Marshall              Lib     7,638   44.0  (-03.6)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
270 (01.5%) informal                   17,369   06.0    03.6 to ALP
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Marshall was Tas MHA for Bass 1914-25.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Jens August Jensen (1856-1936): Elected 1910, 1913, 1914
Assistant Minister 17 September 1914 to 12 July 1915
Minister for the Navy 12 July 1915 to 17 February 1917
Minister for Trade and Customs from 17 February 1917
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Jensen left the ALP in November 1916 and joined the Nationalist 
Party in February 1917.
