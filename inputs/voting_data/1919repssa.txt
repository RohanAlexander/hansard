> George Edwin Yates (1871-1959): Elected 1914 By, 1914, 1917. 
Defeated 1919
Later career: See Adelaide 1922. 
> Reginald Pole Blundell (1873-1945): Elected 1919 
Born: 4 February 1873, Adelaide.
Career: Primary education. Tobacco worker and tobacco worker union 
official. President Adelaide Trades and Labor Council. Left ALP 1916.
State politics: SA MHA for South Adelaide 1907-18. Minister for 
Industry 1915-17, Minister for Marine 1915-17, Minister for 
Repatriation 1917-18, Minister for Industry 1917-18.
