====================================================================

TASMANIA
====================================================================

BASS, Tas                      20,905 enrolled, 11,882 (56.8%) voted
====================================================================
Northern Tasmania: Fingal, Launceston, Ringarooma, Scottsdale
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1906 majority: Protectionist over Anti-Socialist 12.3
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Jens JENSEN                     ALP     6,612   56.8
David Storrer *                         5,022   43.2  
--------------------------------------------------------------------
248 (02.1%) informal                   11,643   06.8
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Storrer was an independent protectionist candidate.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
> David Storrer (1854-1935): Elected 1903, 1906. Defeated 1910
Died 13 November 1935
> Jens August Jensen (1856-1936): Elected 1910 
Born: 1865, Victoria.
Career: Primary education. Orchardist. Beaconsfield Council.
State politics: Tas MHA for George Town 1903-09, Wilmot 1909
--------------------------------------------------------------------

DENISON, Tas                   19,670 enrolled, 12,701 (64.6%) voted
====================================================================
Hobart area: Hobart, Hobart South, New Town, Queenborough
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1906 two-party majority: Anti-Socialist over Labour 10.5
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Matthew Simmons                 Lib     5,172   41.9
William LAIRD SMITH             ALP     7,170   58.1  (+20.5)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
359 (02.8%) informal                   12,342   08.1    18.6 to ALP
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1. The Liberal vote of 41.9% was a decline of 20.5% from the 62.4% 
polled by one Protectionist and one Anti-Socialist candidate in 
1906.
2. Laird Smith appeared second on the ballot paper because his name 
was legally Smith.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
William Henry Laird Smith (1869-1942): Elected 1910
Born: 15 September 1869, Tasmania.
Career: Educated Devonport Grammar School. Electrician, electrical 
engineer. Official, engineers union.
