Robert Wallace Best (1856-1946): Elected 1910 By 
Born: 18 June 1856, Melbourne
Career: Educated Templeton's School, Melbourne. Solicitor 1881. 
Fitzroy Council, Mayor 1888.
Colonial politics: Vic MLA for Fitzroy 1889-1901. President of the 
Board of Land and Works 1894-99, Commissioner of Crown Lands and 
Survey 1894-99, Commissioner of Trade and Customs 1894-99. Knighted 
1908.
Senate: Senator for Victoria 1901-10.
