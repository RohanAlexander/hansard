====================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------

Edgar Dawes                     ALP     19,950   33.9
Albert Edwards                           4,587   07.8
Fred Stacey *                   UAP     29,393   50.0  (+04.7)

3,090 (05.0%) informal                  58,836

1. Davis was an independent Labor candidate.
2. Dawes was SA MHA for Sturt 1930-33.
3. Edwards was SA MHA for Adelaide 1917-31. He was an independent
Labor candidate.
4. The ALP vote of 33.9% was a decrease of 20.8% from the 54.7%
polled by three ALP candidates in 1937.

Archie Galbraith Cameron (1895-1956): Elected 1934, 1937, 1940

Cameron resigned from the Country Party and joined the UAP. He
