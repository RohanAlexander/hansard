Jacob Stumm (1853-1921): Elected 1913
Born: 26 August 1853, Frankfurt-am-Main, Germany.
Career: Migrated as child. Educated state schools Toowoomba. Hansard 
reporter, journalist and newspaper proprietor (Gympie Times). 
Invested in dairying and goldmining.
Colonial politics: Qld MLA for Gympie 1896-99.
