Enid Muriel Lyons (1897-1981): Elected 1943
Born: 9 July 1897, Duck River, Tasmania. Nee Burnell.
Career: Educated Teachers Training College, Hobart. Trainee teacher, 
married at 17. Wife of Rt Hon Joseph Lyons MP. Dame 1937. After 
death of husband, broadcaster and columnist.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Lyons was the first woman elected to the House of Representatives.
