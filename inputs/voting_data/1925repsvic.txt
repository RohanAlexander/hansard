Henry Somer Gullett (1878-1940): Elected 1925
Born: 26 March 1878, Herston, Victoria.
Career: Educated state schools. Journalist, Sydney and Britain. War
correspondent. Military Service 1916-19. Attended Versailles Peace
Conference. Director, Australian War Memorial. Journalist and
author. Father of Henry Baynton Somer Gullett MP.
