COMMONWEALTH OF AUSTRALIA
LEGISLATIVE ELECTION OF 12 DECEMBER 1906
====================================================================

Parties
--------------------------------------------------------------------
* FT - Free Trade
* Lab - Labour
* Prot - Protectionist

VOTING BY CONSTITUENCY
====================================================================

TASMANIA
====================================================================

BASS, Tas                       18,647 enrolled, 9,728 (52.2%) voted
====================================================================
Northern Tasmania: Fingal, Launceston, Ringarooma, Scottsdale
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1903 majority: Protectionist over Free Trade 06.7
--------------------------------------------------------------------
William Oldham                  AS      3,522   37.7  (-05.6)
David STORRER *                 Prot    5,826   62.3  (+05.6)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
380 (03.9%) informal                    9,348   12.3
--------------------------------------------------------------------
There was a swing of 5.6% from Anti-Socialist to Protectionist.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
David Storrer (1854-1935): Elected 1903, 1906
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Storrer refused to support the Protectionist/Anti-Socialist fusion 
in 1908 and sat as an independent from 1909.
-------------------------------------------------------------------

DENISON, Tas                   19,111 enrolled, 11,462 (60.0%) voted
====================================================================
Hobart area: Hobart, Hobart South, New Town, Queenborough
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1903 majority: Protectionist over Free Trade 00.2
--------------------------------------------------------------------
William Brown                             434   03.9

Hon Sir Philip FYSH *           AS      6,473   58.5  (+15.1)
--------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Fysh stood as an Anti-Socialist having previously sat as a 
Protectionist.

independent Protectionist candidate.
3. For Burns, see Illawarra 1913.
