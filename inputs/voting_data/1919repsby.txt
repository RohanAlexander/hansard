--------------------------------------------------------------------
George James Foley (1873-1945): Elected 1920 By 
Born: 1873, Walhalla, Victoria.
Career: Educated state schools, Victoria. To Western Australia 1895, 
goldminer. Active miners union. Left ALP 1916.
State politics: WA MLA for Leonora 1911-20.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
This is the only House of Representatives by-election in which the
