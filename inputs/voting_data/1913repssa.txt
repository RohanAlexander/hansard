> David John Gordon (1865-1946): Elected 1911 By. Defeated 1913
Later career: Company director. SA MLC for Midland 1913-44. Minister 
for Education 1917, Minister for Repatriation 1917. Knighted 1925.

> George Dankel (1864-1926): Elected 1913 
Born: 6 March 1864, Braunschweig, Germany.
Career: Primary education, Germany. Migrated 1879. Butcher, 
Adelaide. 
Norwood Council 1895-1906.
State politics: SA MHA for Torrens 1905-12.
