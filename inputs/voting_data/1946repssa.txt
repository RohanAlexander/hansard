Albert Victor Thompson (1886-1966): Elected 1946
Born: 14 November 1886, Adelaide.
Career: Educated public schools. Dairy farmer. Later carter, 
President Carters and Drivers Union. President, Port Adelaide Trades 
and Labor Council. President, South Australian ALP.
State politics: SA MHA for Port Adelaide 1930-38, Semaphore 1938-46.
Thompson contested Port Adelaide in 1949.
