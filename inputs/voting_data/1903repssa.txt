James Hutchison (1859-1909): Elected 1903
Born: 20 April 1859, Aberdeen, Scotland.
Career: Educated Mechanics Institute, Aberdeen. Printer and 
journalist. Migrated 1884. Compositor and publisher, Adelaide. 
President South Australian Labour Party.
Colonial/State politics: SA MHA for East Adelaide 1898-1902.
