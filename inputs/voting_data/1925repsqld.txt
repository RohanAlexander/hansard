KENNEDY, Qld                                         33,277 enrolled
====================================================================
North Queensland: Atherton, Charters Towers, Cloncurry, Longreach
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1922 two-party majority: ALP over Country Party 11.6
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Grosvenor FRANCIS               Nat     Unopposed
--------------------------------------------------------------------
The sitting member since 1901, Hon Charles McDonald (ALP), who was
the only other candidate, died 13 November 1925, between the close
of nominations and polling day. Francis was declared elected
unopposed. The Electoral Act was later amended to provide for the
cancellation of the poll and the re-opening of nominations in these
circumstances.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Grosvenor Arundell Francis (1873-1944): Elected 1925
Born: 14 April 1873, Normanton, Queensland.
Career: Educated Brisbane Grammar School, University of Queensland.
Barrister 1901. Practised Charters Towers 1901-26.
