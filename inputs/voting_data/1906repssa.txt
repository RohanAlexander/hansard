John Livingston (1856-1935): Elected 1906
Born: 24 March 1856, Mt Gambier, South Australia.
Career: Primary education. Drover, stock dealer and station manager, 
NSW, Victoria and Queensland. Mayor of Mt Gambier 1898. 
Colonial/State politics: SA MHA for Victoria 1899-1902, Victoria and 
Albert 1902-06.
