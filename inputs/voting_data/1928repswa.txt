John Curtin (1885-1945): Elected 1928
Born: 8 January 1885, Creswick, Victoria.
Career: Educated state schools, Melbourne. Factory worker, labor 
journalist. Briefly imprisoned as anti-conscriptionist. Secretary 
Timberworkers Union. To Western Australia 1917. Labor editor 
1917-28.

Edward Alexander Mann (1874-1951): Elected 1922, 1925, 1928
--------------------------------------------------------------------
On 10 September 1929 Mann was one of four Nationalist members who 
voted in committee against the Maritime Services Bill, leading to 
the fall of the Government. He was expelled from the Nationalist 
Party as a result.
