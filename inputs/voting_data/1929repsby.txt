Edward John Ward (1899-1963): Elected 1931 By
Born: 7 March 1899, Sydney.
Career: Educated state schools. Laborer, boilermaker, clerk.
Tramways worker, dismissed for striking. Sydney City Council.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Ward was an endorsed candidate of the NSW ALP, but he campaigned in
opposition to the Scullin Government's policies and was not
supported by the ALP's federal leaders. As a result he was not
admitted to the federal ALP Caucus. This precipitated the split
between the NSW ALP and the federal party. Ward voted against the
Scullin Government in the censure motion of 26 November 1931.
