Herbert Victor Johnson (1889-1962): Elected 1940
Born: 25 October 1889, Northampton, Western Australia.
Career: Educated Catholic schools. Stationhand, shearer. Organiser,

Thomas William Marwick (1895-1960): Elected 1940 By

Archibald Grenfell Price (1892-1977): Elected 1941 By
Born: 28 January 1892, Adelaide.
Career: Educated St Peters College and Oxford University, DLitt. 
House master, St Peters College. Master, St Marks College, 
University of Adelaide 1925-57. Chairman of the Emergency Committee 
of South Australia 1931-32. Not related to the previous member.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Price joined the "National Service" group under the leadership of
