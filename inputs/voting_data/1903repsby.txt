WILMOT, Tas                     15,718 enrolled, 4,704 (29.9%) voted
====================================================================
Central Tasmania: Deloraine, Devonport, Kentish, Longford
--------------------------------------------------------------------
26 February 1904, following the death of Rt Hon Sir Edward Braddon
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1903 majority: Free Trade over Protectionist 04.9
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Donald CAMERON                  FT      2,368   52.0  (-02.9)
John Cheek                      Prot    2,183   48.0  (+02.9)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
153 (03.3%) informal                    4,551   02.0
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1. There was a swing of 2.9% from Free Trade to Protectionist.
2. Cameron was a member for Tasmania 1901-03.
3. Cheek was Tas MLC for Westmorland 1907-13, 1919-42.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Donald Norman Cameron (1851-1931): Elected 1901. Defeated 1903.

William Robert Nuttal Maloney (1854-1940): Elected 1904 By 
Born: 12 April 1854, Melbourne.
Career: Educated Scotch College, Melbourne and English medical
