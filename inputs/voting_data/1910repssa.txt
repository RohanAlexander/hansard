William Oliver Archibald (1852-1926): Elected 1910
Born: 3 June 1852, London, England.
Career: Primary education. Migrated 1881. Railwayman. Chairman Port 
Adelaide Workingmens' Association. Founding member Labor Party.
Colonial/State politics: SA MHA for Port Adelaide 1893-1910.
