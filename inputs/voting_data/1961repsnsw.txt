Page was 81 and was too ill to campaign: he had ignored local advice 
to retire. He died without knowing that he had lost his seat. He was 
member for Cowper for 42 years, the longest period for which a 
member of the House of Representatives has held a single seat (Billy 
Hughes was a member for 51 years, but held four different seats). 
Since he was defeated, he technically ceased to be a member of 
Parliament on 2 November 1961, the day the Twenty-Third Parliament 
was dissolved.
