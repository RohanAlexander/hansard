Parties
--------------------------------------------------------------------

QUEENSLAND
====================================================================

BRISBANE, Qld                  34,142 enrolled, 17,466 (51.2%) voted
====================================================================
Inner Brisbane: Brisbane North, Fortitude Valley, Toowong, Windsor
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1901 two-party majority: Protectionist over Labour 07.6
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Dr Millice CULPIN               Lab     8,019   46.8  (+15.1)
Thomas Macdonald-Paterson *             1,799   10.5         
William Morse                   NLU     7,321   42.7   
--------------------------------------------------------------------
327 (01.9%) informal                   17,139   02.1    09.7 to Lab
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Morse having secured the NLU endorsement, Macdonald-Paterson, 
though nominally a protectionist, was in effect an independent since 
there was no protectionist organisation in Queensland.
2. The Labour majority over the NLU is compared with the 
Protectionist majority over Labour in 1901.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
> Thomas Macdonald-Paterson (1844-1906): Elected 1901. 
Defeated 1903 
Died 21 March 1906
> Millice Culpin (1846-1941): Elected 1903
Born: 1 December 1846, Hertfordshire, England.
Career: Educated Alleynes Grammar School, England, and University of 
Edinburgh. Doctor. Migrated 1891.
--------------------------------------------------------------------

David Alexander Thomson (1856-1926): Elected 1903
Born: 26 February 1856, Melbourne.
Career: Educated state schools. Goldminer, Victoria. Shopkeeper, 
publican, NSW and Queensland. Rockhampton 1896, cordial manufacturer 
and farmer.
