--------------------------------------------------------------------
> Frederick Albert McDonald (1872-?1925): Elected 1922. Defeated
1925
Died: ?1925
> Thomas John Ley (1879-1947): Elected 1925
Born: 28 October 1879, Bath, England.
Career: Migrated as child. Primary education. Studied law while
working, solicitor. Company director.
State politics: NSW MLA for Hurstville 1917-25. Minister for Public
Instruction 1921, Minister for Labour and Industry 1921, Minister
for Justice 1922-25.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
McDonald challenged Ley's election but disappeared soon after. He
was probably murdered. In 1947 Ley (who was insane) was convicted of
murder in Britain, and it seems likely that he was also responsible
for McDonald's death.
