--------------------------------------------------------------------
Charles Allan Seymour Hawker (1894-1938): Elected 1929, 1931
Minister for Markets 6 January 1932 to 13 April 1932
Minister for Repatriation 6 January 1932 to 12 April 1932
Minister for Commerce 13 April 1932 to 23 September 1932
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Hawker had given a commitment during the election that he would vote
for a reduction in parliamentary salaries. When Moses Gabb
(Independent, Angas) moved a motion to reduce salaries (which the
government opposed), Hawker resigned from the ministry so that he
could vote for it.
